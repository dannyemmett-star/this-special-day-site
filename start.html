Step 2: Complete onboarding

After payment, customers should complete a short onboarding form with business details, preferred sender name, reply-to email, BCC/copy email, magazine page tone, permission to email contacts, and contact list upload.

  • Business or professional name
  • Preferred sender name
  • Preferred reply email address
  • BCC/copy email address for invitation records
  • Customer CSV or Excel file with first name, email, birthday month, and birthday day
  • Permission to email the uploaded contacts on behalf of the business
  • Any tone or relationship notes for the magazine pages

The list does not need to be in a perfect column order. Extra columns are okay, and no birth year is needed.
