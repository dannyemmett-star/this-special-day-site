What It Is

More Than a Birthday Email

This Special Day is a relationship-building system that helps businesses show up on one of the most personal days of the year: a customer's birthday.

Instead of sending a generic "Happy Birthday" note, each recipient receives an invitation to a personalized birthday magazine page created just for them. The page connects their birthday to meaningful events from that date in history, culture, science, sports, music, film, business, or world events.

The result is a birthday experience that feels thoughtful, memorable, and different.

FAQ

Good questions before you begin.

Is this just a birthday email service?

No. The email is only the invitation. This Special Day creates personalized birthday magazine pages designed to help your business stay remembered.

Do invitations come from my business?

Email invitations are sent through This Special Day with your business or personal name displayed. Replies go back to your preferred email address.

Will I receive a copy?

Yes. You receive a copy of each invitation sent and a send report.

Can I upload my own contacts?

Yes. You upload a CSV contact list with names, emails, and birthdays.

What format does my contact list need?

The basic format is First Name, Last Name, Email, Birthday Month, and Birthday Date. No birth year is needed.

Is my customer data private?

Yes. Your contact list is used only to prepare and send Special Day magazine page invitations for your account.

Can I cancel?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription anytime.

Do I have to write the messages?

No. This Special Day creates the magazine page, event connection, image or visual, and birthday message for you.
